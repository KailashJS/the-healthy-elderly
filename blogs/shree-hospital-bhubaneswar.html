Shree Hospital Bhubaneswar

Author Kailash Chandra Bhakta

Shree Hospital is a 100 bedded Multi-Speciality Hospital with all state-of-the-art facilities in Bhubaneswar at an affordable cost.

The Hospital has been established and started operation on 1st August 2013 and within such a short period, This has been ranked as the 5th best orthopaedic and trauma care hospital in Odisha, in the year 2016, second-best in 2017 and as of now it is ranked as the No-1 Orthopedic Hospital in the state of Odisha as per the Times Health survey 2018.

It has the best state-of-the-art technologies and facilities for:

  • Orthopedic
  • Oncology
  • Neurology
  • Neuro Surgery
  • General Medicine and Critical Care
  • Gynecology & Obstetrics
  • Neonatology & Paediatrics
  • Nephrology
  • Urology
  • Dermatology
  • Gastrology etc.
